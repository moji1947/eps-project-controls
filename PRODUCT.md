# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary users are project managers overseeing active construction programmes. They need a fast, dependable view across progress, quality, cost, and delivery so they can identify risks and coordinate corrective action.

## Product Purpose

EPS Construction Dashboard is a frontend-only project-control workspace that brings project health, schedule signals, quality issues, financial exposure, and delivery status into one working view. Success means a project manager can understand the state of a project, spot what needs attention, and move to the relevant workstream without hunting through reports.

## Positioning

One clear operational picture for construction project managers—connecting schedule, cost, quality, and delivery risks in the same workspace.

## Brand Personality

Modern, orderly, and dependable. The interface should feel familiar to experienced project managers: information-rich without feeling chaotic, direct without being cold, and calm when the project is not.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS dashboards, novelty navigation, excessive color, and over-stylized data visualization. This should be a trustworthy operations tool, not a marketing surface.

## Design Principles

- Surface the exception before the detail.
- Keep related operational signals in the same decision context.
- Use familiar controls and readable data density.
- Make status meaning clear without relying on color alone.
- Preserve a calm, keyboard-friendly workflow under pressure.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.1 AA contrast requirements, support keyboard navigation and visible focus states, avoid color-only status communication, and respect reduced-motion preferences.
